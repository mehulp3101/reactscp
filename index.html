What is SCP

"SCPs" basically refers to anomalies, basically unnatural or supernatural phenomenons, creatures, ideas, events, and anything in-between that just shouldn't exist in the real world, but does anyways. In the universes of the SCP wiki, these supernatural monsters and things are called "SCPs" because they each have their own "Special Containment Procedures", which is where the term "SCP" comes from. The general gist of the universe is about this secret organization called "The SCP Foundation" whose mission it is to contain SCPs to keep the world safe and prevent people from finding out about the supernatural and the anomalous.
